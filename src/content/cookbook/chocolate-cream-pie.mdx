---
title: Chocolate Cream Pie
description: This dairy-free chocolate cream pie has a rich filling that pairs perfectly with a pre-made, savory crust.
source: https://www.onelovelylife.com/vegan-chocolate-pie/
pubDate: 2024-07-03
author: David
image:
tags: [pie, chocolate, dessert, dairy-free]

servings: 12 slices
prepTime: 10 minutes
cookTime: 30 minutes
totalTime: 40 minutes

ingredients:
  - category: Crust
    items:
      - 1 pre-made, 9 inch pie crust
  - category: Chocolate Cream Filling
    items:
      - 3 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder
      - 4 tablespoons cornstarch
      - ⅓ cup sugar
      - 1 13 ounce can full-fat coconut milk
      - 1 cup unsweetened alternative milk
      - ⅔ cup dairy-free chocolate chips
      - 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
      - ⅛ teaspoon salt
  - category: Dairy-free Whipping Cream
    items:
      - 1 403 milliliter can full fat coconut milk, chilled overnight
      - ½ teaspoon unflavored powdered gelatin, or agar agar powder
      - 1 tablespoon raw honey, or maple syrup

instructions:
  - Bake the pie crust according to package instructions. Allow to cool completely
  - In a medium saucepan, whisk together the cocoa powder, cornstarch, and sugar. Add the coconut milk and alternative milk
  - Whisk constantly over medium heat until the mixture thickens and comes to a boil. Boil for 2-3 minute, or until very thick, then remove from heat
  - Add the chocolate chips, vanilla, and salt. Stir until the chocolate is melted and the mixture is smooth
  - Pour the filling into the cold pie crust and chill for at least 4 hours, or until set
  - To make the whipping cream, open the can of chilled coconut milk and scoop out the thick cream on top. Whip the cream with a hand mixer until fluffy. Add the gelatin and honey and whip until well combined
