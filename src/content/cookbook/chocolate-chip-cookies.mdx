servings: 36 cookies
prepTime: 20 minutes
cookTime: 8 minutes
totalTime: 28 minutes

ingredients:
  - category: Dough
    items:
      - 1 cup salted dairy-free butter, softened
      - 1 cup granulated sugar
      - 1 cup light brown sugar, packed
      - 2 teaspoons pure vanilla extract
      - 2 large eggs
      - 3 cups all-purpose flour
      - 1 teaspoon baking soda
      - ½ teaspoon baking powder
      - 1 teaspoon sea salt
      - 2 cups chocolate chips

instructions:
  - Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C). Line baking sheets, 3-4, with parchment paper
  - Mix flour, baking soda, baking powder, and salt. Set aside
  - Cream butter and sugars together until light and fluffy
  - Beat in eggs and vanilla, about 1 minute
  - Mix in dry ingredients until just combined
  - Stir in chocolate chips
  - Roll dough into balls and evenly space on baking sheets. Flatten slightly
  - Bake for 8-10 minutes, or until edges are set and golden brown
