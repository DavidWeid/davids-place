title: Marry Me Tofu
description: This creamy tofu dish is easy to make and full of flavor. It’s perfect for a weeknight dinner or a special occasion.
source: https://itdoesnttastelikechicken.com/marry-me-tofu/
pubDate:
author: David
image:
tags:
  - vegan
  - tofu
  - creamy

servings: 4-6 servings
prepTime: 15 minutes
cookTime: 25 minutes
totalTime: 40 minutes

ingredients:
  - category: Tofu
    items:
      - 1 blocks extra-firm tofu
      - 3 all-purpose flour
      - ¼ teaspoon salt
      - ¼ teaspoon black pepper
  - category: Sauce
    items:
      - 2 tablespoons olive oil
      - 4 garlic cloves, minced
      - 1 cup broth
      - 1 cup culinary cream or coconut cream
      - 2 tablespoons nutritional yeast
      - 1 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes
      - ½ teaspoon oregano
      - ¼ teaspoon thyme
      - ¼ teaspoon salt
      - ⅓ cup sun-dried tomatoes, or roasted red peppers
      - 1 handful fresh basil, chopped

instructions:
  - Add the tofu, flour, salt, and pepper to a medium bowl and gently toss to coat the tofu
  - Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the tofu and cook until golden brown on all sides. Remove the tofu and set aside
  - Return the pan to the stove and reduce heat to medium. Add garlic and cook for 1 minute. Pour in the broth and scrape up any browned bits from the bottom of the pan
  - Add in the cream, nutritional yeast, red pepper flakes, oregano, thyme, and salt. Stir to combine and bring to a simmer to cook for a few minutes
  - Add back the tofu with the sauce and stir in the sun-dried tomatoes or roasted red peppers. Cook for a few more minutes until everything is heated through
  - Garnish with basil and serve over rice
