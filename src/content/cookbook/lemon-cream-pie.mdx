title: Lemon Cream Pie

tags: [pie, lemon, dessert, dairy-free]

servings: 12 slices
prepTime: 40 minutes
cookTime: 15 minutes
totalTime: 55 minutes

ingredients:
  - category: Crust
    items:
      - 2 cups (228 grams) blanched almond flour
      - 2 teaspoons (4 grams) coconut flour
      - 4 tablespoons (46 grams) coconut oil, melted
      - 4 tablespoons (100 grams) raw honey
      - ⅛ teaspoon salt
  - category: Lemon Cream Filling
    items:
      - ½ cup lemon juice
      - 3 tablespoons lemon zest
      - 1 403 milliliter can full fat coconut milk
      - 2 cups (300 grams) raw cashews, soaked in hot water for an hour
      - 8 tablespoons (190g) raw honey
      - 4½ teaspoons (10g) unflavored powdered gelatin, or agar agar powder
      - 1 tablespoons vanilla extract
  - category: Dairy-free Whipping Cream
    items:
      - 1 403 milliliter can full fat coconut milk, chilled overnight
      - ½ teaspoon unflavored powdered gelatin, or agar agar powder
      - 1 tablespoon raw honey, or maple syrup

instructions:
  - Preheat oven to 350°F. Mix all the crust ingredients together in a bowl until well combined. Press the dough into a 9-inch pie dish and bake until the edges and center start to brown. Remove from the oven and let it cool
  - In a saucepan, add the lemon juice, lemon zest, and half of the coconut milk. Sprinkle the gelatin on top and allow to soften
  - In a blender, add the cashews, remaining coconut milk, honey, and vanilla. Blend until very creamy and smooth
  - Heat the lemon mixture over low-medium heat until the gelatin is dissolved. Cool for 5 minutes and then add to the blender with the cashew mixture. Blend until smooth
  - Allow mixture to cool, then pour into the cooled pie crust. Refrigerate for at least 6 hours or until set
  - To make the whipping cream, open the can of chilled coconut milk and scoop out the thick cream on top. Whip the cream with a hand mixer until fluffy. Add the gelatin and honey and whip until well combined
