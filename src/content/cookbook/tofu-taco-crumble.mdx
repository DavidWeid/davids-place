---
title: Tofu Taco Crumble
description: A quick and easy tofu taco crumble recipe. This is a great filling for tacos, burritos, or salads.
source: https://itdoesnttastelikechicken.com/vegan-tofu-taco-crumbles/
author: David
image:
  url: '@images/cookbook/tofu-taco-crumble/tofu-tacos.jpg'
  alt: Tofu taco crumble for taco night
tags: [tacos, tofu, mexican, quick-meal, vegan, dairy-free]

servings: 6 tacos
prepTime: 10 minutes
cookTime: 40 minutes
totalTime: 50 minutes

ingredients:
  - category: Sauce
    items:
      - 1 tablespoon oil
      - 1 tablespoon soy sauce
      - 2 teaspoons chili powder
      - 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
      - ½ teaspoon cumin
      - ½ teaspoon onion powder
      - ½ teaspoon garlic powder
      - ¼ teaspoon cayenne pepper
  - category: Other
    items:
      - 1 block extra-firm tofu
      - ¾+ cup salsa
      - Onion
      - Bell pepper
      - Cilantro, rice, guacamole, cheese, etc for serving

instructions:
  - Preheat oven to 350°F (180°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper
  - Mix the oil, soy sauce, and spices in a bowl to make a paste-like texture
  - Crumble the tofu into the bowl and mix well to coat the tofu with the paste
  - Spread the tofu mixture evenly on the baking sheet and bake for 25-35 minutes, stirring halfway through, until browned
  - Remove from oven and add to pan with salsa, onion, and bell pepper. Cook until heated through
